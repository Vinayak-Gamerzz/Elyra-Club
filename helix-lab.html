Helix Lab

Advanced Robotics, Automation & Hardware Innovation

Helix Lab is the experimental core of Elyra where ideas turn into real machines. From robotics and automation to smart systems and engineering builds, this space focuses on practical innovation and real-world creation. Members explore electronics, intelligent devices, sensors and futuristic hardware projects while learning through hands-on experimentation.
